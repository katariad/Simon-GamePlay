How to Play

Objective: Your goal is to follow the sequence of colors and sounds generated by Simon.

Setup: Simon features four colored buttons - red, blue, green, and yellow. The game begins with Simon displaying a single color and sound.

Gameplay:

  1. Watch & Listen: Pay attention as Simon reveals the sequence by lighting up the buttons and playing sounds.
  2. Repeat the Sequence: Click on the colored buttons in the same order as Simon's sequence.
  3. Level Up: Successfully repeat the sequence to advance to the next level. Simon adds a new color and sound to the sequence with each successful round.
  4. Challenge Yourself: Keep pace as Simon increases the length and speed of the sequence, testing your memory and reflexes.

User Input: Click on the colored buttons to mimic Simon's sequence. Each button press lights up the color and plays the corresponding sound.

Scoring: Your score is based on the number of rounds completed successfully. Aim for a high score by reaching as many levels as possible.

Game Over:

  • If you make a mistake by clicking the wrong button or pressing them out of sequence, the game ends. Listen for a buzzing sound to indicate an error.
  • Simon will display the correct sequence, allowing you to learn from your mistake and try again.

Restart: Feel free to restart the game at any time to challenge yourself anew. Simply press the restart button to begin again.

Difficulty: As you progress, Simon will present longer and faster sequences, putting your memory and concentration to the test.
